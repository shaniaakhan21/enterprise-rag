Acme Corporation reported total revenue of $4.82 billion for fiscal year 2023.
This represents an 11.8% increase compared to $4.31 billion in fiscal year 2022.

Net income for 2023 was $930 million with a net income margin of 19.3%.
The company employed 8,742 full-time employees globally as of December 2023.

Cash and cash equivalents stood at $2.14 billion on the balance sheet.
Capital expenditures for the year were $187 million.
